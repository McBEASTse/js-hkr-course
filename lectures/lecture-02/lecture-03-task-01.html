My main hobby - Pinball

I started playing pinball about four years ago. What I like about this hobby is both learning a new skill like controlling chaos (the ball), aiming (shooting the ball where you want), there is no limit of how good you can get (there is always a high-score to beat) and the people you meet.

There is a lot more to say about pinball but it's a great hobby both for the competative person, the tinkerer or just if you have infinite money to spend (buying pinball machines is not cheap).
